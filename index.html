Our idea :

OThe idea is to create one of the best business schools in France by giving post-baccalaureate students the opportunity to have access to the best international teachers. The project is to create a school in the center of the city which is the opposite of the big Parisian campuses in order to allow students to benefit from a campus close to all aspects. This allows us to be close to train stations, close to parks and to have classrooms.

How to choose?

Today, IESEG offers you many possibilities. A course completely in English in order to adapt your speech to the international community. The possibility of doing internships and a stay abroad in a partner university. A complete choice of partners to find the internship of your dreams. A multiple choice of master's degree in order to find one's path and to give oneself every chance.
